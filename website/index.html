Welcome! Web3 games finally make it to the outer reaches with Space Wars: Diplomacy, a fully on-chain strategy game with innovative resource management, built by the Global Hackers of theChainlink Hackathon. It features all the classic abilities from 4x games you love, like moving, shooting, and trading, but stretched out over a galatic timescale! For playtesting and the alpha phase, the timescale may change.... but read on to find the Rules.

FAQ

Where do I get a spaceship?

Join our Discord to be part of the first set of spaceships granted. Colors and locations are randomized, but all ships are equally powerful.

The game will begin with an auction of all spaceships, randomly allocated across the reaches of deep space. Every day, your ship will accumulate an action point, which you can use to upgrade your ship, move, or attack another player. You can also trade these points with other players, contribute them to an alliance, or simply let them accumulate, but be careful: Once your shield points drop to zero, you will lose the ability to accumulate any additional energy because your reactor has been destroyed.
